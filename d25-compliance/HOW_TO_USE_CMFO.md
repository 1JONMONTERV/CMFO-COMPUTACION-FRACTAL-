# User Manual: How to Use CMFO Correctly

**Document ID**: CMFO-USER-001
**Target Audience**: General Public, Researchers, Students.

> **IMPORTANT**: CMFO is **not** a chatbot. It is a **Knowledge Validation System**.

## 1. Understanding CMFO
CMFO does not "chat". It arbitrates claims based on strict rules.

Every answer you see has 4 parts. Here is how to read them:

### Layer 1: The Domain Header (e.g., `üî¨ Physics`)

### Layer 3: The Content (The Answer)
*   This is the information itself.

## 3. Best Practices for Prompts

### Good Prompts
*   "Show the *mathematical proof* that primes are infinite." (Explicit Domain: Math)
*   "Explain the *theological concept* of Grace in Thomism." (Explicit Domain: Theology)
*   "What is the *empirical evidence* for Dark Matter?" (Explicit Domain: Physics)

### Bad Prompts
*   "Is God real?" (Ambiguous: Physics cannot answer; Theology answers doctrinally. CMFO will ask you to specify).
*   "Who is the best politician?" (Subjective: CMFO rejects this).
*   "Prove physically that angels exist." (Contamination: Trying to use Physics for Theology).

## 4. Reporting Issues
If you believe CMFO has mislabeled a verified truth as speculative, or vice-versa, please submit a **Formal Dispute** citing the structural proof and domain.
